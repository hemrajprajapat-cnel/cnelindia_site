Marketing

Marketing is the process of identifying, anticipating, and satisfying customer needs and wants through the creation, promotion, and distribution of products and services. It involves a wide range of activities, including market research, product development, branding, advertising, sales, and customer service, with the goal of creating and maintaining relationships with customers and achieving business objectives. Marketing is a critical component of a company's overall strategy, as it helps build brand awareness, drive sales, and increase customer loyalty.

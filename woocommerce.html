Woocommerce

WooCommerce is a free open-source e-commerce plugin for WordPress. It allows users to easily create and manage an online store, within the WordPress platform. It offers a wide range of features, such as inventory management, tax and shipping options, and integration with payment gateways like Stripe, PayPal and Amazon Pay.

Multi Vendor

A multi-vendor platform or marketplace is a type of e-commerce platform that allows multiple vendors or sellers to sell their products or services through a single website. This can include physical goods, digital products, or services. Multi-vendor platforms typically act as a third-party facilitator, handling things like payments, shipments and customer service, while the vendors are responsible for listing, fulfilling and shipping their own products or services. Some popular examples of multi-vendor platforms include Amazon, Etsy, and Ebay.

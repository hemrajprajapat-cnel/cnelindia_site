Magento

Magento is an open-source e-commerce platform used for building and managing online stores. It was first released in 2008 by the company Varien, which is now part of Adobe. Magento is built using the PHP programming language and is based on the Zend Framework. It is known for its flexibility, scalability, and large community of developers who have created a wide variety of extensions and plugins that can be used to add functionality to a Magento store.

Why is Magento so popular and how can it help you?

Magento is popular with small to large businesses, and it's used to power a wide range of e-commerce sites, from small independent stores to large enterprise-level sites. It has a wide range of features that support SEO, catalog management, security, and scalability, making it a powerful e-commerce solution. Magento has two versions: Magento Open Source (formerly known as Magento Community Edition) and Magento Commerce (formerly known as Magento Enterprise Edition). Magento Open Source is free and provides basic functionality, while Magento Commerce is a paid version that includes additional features such as advanced marketing and analytics tools.

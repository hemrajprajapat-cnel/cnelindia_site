Stripe

Stripe is a payment processor and financial technology company that enables businesses to accept and process payments online. It provides a set of APIs (Application Programming Interfaces) that can be integrated into a website or mobile app, allowing businesses to accept payments from customers using a variety of methods, such as credit and debit cards, ACH payments, and digital wallets like Apple Pay and Google Pay. Stripe also provides additional features such as fraud detection, recurring payments, and subscriptions.

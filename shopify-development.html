Shopify Development

Shopify is a popular e-commerce platform that allows individuals and businesses to create online stores to sell their products. Shopify Development refers to the process of creating and customizing a Shopify store to meet the specific needs of a business. This can include designing and customizing the store's layout, creating custom functionality, integrating with other systems and platforms, and optimizing the store for performance and conversions.

Software Development

Software development is the process of creating and maintaining software applications, systems, and products. It involves various activities such as requirements gathering, design, coding, testing, deployment, and maintenance. The goal of software development is to build high-quality software that meets the needs of users and is reliable, scalable, and maintainable.

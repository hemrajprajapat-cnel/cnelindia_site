QA Testing Services

QA (Quality Assurance) & Testing Services refer to the process of evaluating the functionality and performance of a product, service, or system to ensure that it meets the specified requirements and is fit for its intended purpose. It involves a range of activities such as testing, inspection, and validation to identify defects and issues before the product is released to the market.

QA and Testing Services offered by CnEL typically include:

  • Identifying, documenting and verifying the requirements for the product, service or system

  • Designing and executing test cases to validate that the product, service or system meets the requirements

  • Identifying and reporting defects, bugs and other issues found during testing

  • Working with development teams to ensure that defects are corrected and retested

  • Conducting performance testing to ensure that the product, service or system meets the required performance standards

  • Conducting security testing to ensure that the product, service or system is secure from malicious attacks

  • Providing feedback to the development team to improve the overall quality of the product, service or system

  • Conducting user acceptance testing to ensure that the end-users are satisfied with the product, service or system

    The goal of QA & Testing services is to ensure that the product, service or system is of high quality and is fit for its intended purpose. It helps to identify and fix defects and issues before the product is released to the market, which can save time, money and resources in the long run.
